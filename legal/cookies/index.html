Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. They are commonly used to remember preferences, maintain sessions, and collect analytics data. Some cookies are set by the website you visit directly (first-party cookies); others are set by external services embedded in the page (third-party cookies).

2. First-Party Cookies

EcoViewer does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use cookies for analytics, session management, advertising, or any other purpose on our end.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Certain third-party services embedded in or linked from EcoViewer may set their own cookies. These include:

  • Google Fonts — used to load typefaces on this website. Google may collect request data per their standard practices. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Earth Engine — the underlying platform powering the EcoViewer app. When you interact with the embedded app or visit the Earth Engine application directly, Google may set cookies as part of their platform. See Google Earth Engine Terms and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — our hosting provider. GitHub may collect standard web request data. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

We do not control these third-party cookies and are not responsible for them. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those providers directly.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block third-party cookies, clear existing cookies, or receive a notification before a cookie is set. Note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of embedded services like the EcoViewer app.

For guidance on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if new third-party services are added to the platform. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
