// What is EcoViewer

Biodiversity & environmental
context, anywhere

EcoViewer brings together GBIF species occurrence data and global environmental datasets into a single, unified reference platform. Designed for ecologists, remote sensing specialists, and conservation practitioners who need reliable ecological context for landscape analysis, imagery annotation, and biodiversity research.

About the project →
◎

GBIF Integration

Ecosystem indicator species occurrence records from GBIF, matched to ecosystem types and biomes using peer-reviewed ecological literature.

⬡

Environmental Layers

Climate, vegetation, water, and fire data harmonised from verified global repositories into a single queryable reference.

◈

Global Reach

High-resolution coverage across all biomes and continents, powered by Google Earth Engine's planetary-scale infrastructure.

⬕

Open Access

No login, no licence fees. Freely accessible for scientific, educational, and conservation use anywhere in the world.
